Introduction

While colonizing the Americas, European explorers purposefully spread diseases completely foreign to the land’s natives. The Native Americans had no time to build immune defenses to these often deliberately galvanized pathogens and thus suffered heavily.

 We, the authors, set out to see if the spread of disease was caused more by European colonists or by regular disease spread. 

Project Created in 2019 by Michelle Patten, Paul Riddle, Pavan Tauh and Ben Wise
