Ben Wise

Ben Wise is a sophomore Eugene McDermott Scholar studying visual and performing arts and political science at the University of Texas at Dallas.  He is interested in the historical role of the fine arts in social movements. In this project, he attempts to capture how research on the Columbian Exchange contributes to common misperceptions of Indigenous groups. On campus, he is a defensive specialist on the men’s volleyball team, and performs in Chamber Singers and UTD’s premiere a cappella group, Novis. He is also in his first season in the Dallas Symphony Chorus, the official vocal ensemble of the Dallas Symphony Orchestra.  He is a principal baritone in Comet Opera, UTD’s student opera company, and looks forward to being the organization’s first choirmaster. In the future, he will likely pursue a career in choral music education or education policy.
